Taco trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos were introduced to the US by Mexican immigrants in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, but they didn't gain widespread popularity until the 1950s and 1960s, particularly with the opening of the first Taco Bell restaurant in 1962.
